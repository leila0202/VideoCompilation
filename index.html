Charlotte Trailer

"Charlotte" is an anime set in a world where a comet dust causes a small percentage of children to develop superhuman abilities during puberty. The story follows Yuu Otosaka, who can briefly possess others, and Nao Tomori, who can become invisible. They are drawn to Hoshinoumi Academy, a school for these ability users, and must protect them from those who want to exploit their powers.

Plastic Memories

Plastic Memories" is a Japanese anime that revolves around a young man named Tsukasa Mizugaki who joins a company that deals with retrieving "Giftias," androids that look and act like humans. He is paired with Isla, a Giftia, and they work together to help Giftias with their end-of-life processes. The story explores the emotional impact of this work and the relationships that develop between humans and Giftias, with a focus on the bittersweet nature of memories and the end of life.

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling follows Sung Jinwoo, the weakest Hunter, who discovers a mysterious system granting him the ability to level up by fighting monsters. This system allows him to transform from the weakest Hunter to one of the strongest, battling both monsters and fellow humans, ultimately discovering the secrets of his powers and the dungeons.
